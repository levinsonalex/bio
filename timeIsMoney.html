Overview

An small web-applet that shows realtime money gains based on your submitted wage. Fill out the text box with your hourly wage. Then press the "Start Cash Clock" button, and watch as the cash earned text increases over time.

I came upon the idea after a co-worker joked they timed their bathroom breaks to see how much money they earned in the bathroom. So I made this as a small project to practice my javascript skills.

Version 1.0

I created version 1.0 during MHacks 2015. I needed a small project to create within 24 hours, so I finally bought my own domain levinsonalex.com and setup my github pages to host onto it, and then I used the remaining time to create this web-app.
